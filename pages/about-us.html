Who We Are

At Beaches Travel Inc., we believe that traveling is not just about visiting new places, but about discovering new perspectives, experiencing rich cultures, and finding your personal paradise. Based in El Nido, Palawan, our team of dedicated local experts handpick the finest tropical beach destinations, premium resorts, and hidden island excursions to give you a hassle-free vacation of a lifetime.
